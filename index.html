Our French Delicacies

Coq au Vin

Traditional French chicken braised in red wine with mushrooms, onions, and bacon. A classic comfort dish that embodies the essence of French cooking.

Bouillabaisse

A traditional Provençal fish stew originating from Marseille. Fresh seafood simmered in aromatic broth with saffron and herbs.

Ratatouille

A vibrant vegetable medley from Provence featuring eggplant, zucchini, peppers, and tomatoes, slowly cooked with aromatic herbs.

Escargots de Bourgogne

Classic French snails prepared with garlic, parsley, and butter. A delicate appetizer that showcases French culinary finesse.

Crème Brûlée

Rich vanilla custard topped with caramelized sugar. Our signature dessert that provides the perfect sweet ending to your meal.

French Onion Soup

Caramelized onions in rich beef broth, topped with melted Gruyère cheese and toasted bread. A warming classic French soup.

About La Vie est Belle

Welcome to La Vie est Belle, where "Life is Beautiful" becomes a reality through exquisite French cuisine. Located in the vibrant city of Gaborone, we bring the authentic flavors of France to Botswana. Our chef-curated menu features traditional French dishes prepared with locally sourced ingredients and time-honored techniques. Every meal is a celebration of the joie de vivre that defines French culture.
